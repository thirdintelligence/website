## Evaluation rule

Before moving a future feature into the build:

1. confirm the client-retention value;
2. define privacy and tenancy behavior;
3. determine whether individual identity is required;
4. define source of truth and rollback;
5. approve design/copy through HITL;
6. add tests and traceability;
7. update this masterplan and the portal schema version.
